Core Compliance Automation Software

AutoGen-CM & EM

Automated minerals compliance software. Collects supplier reports, validates data structures, and compiles enterprise-level reports.

• Single-Click Collection: Automated supply chain outreach.
• 150+ Validation Rules: Instant error detections and alters
• Audit-Trail Logs: Secured smelter logs for due diligence tracing.

MDS Xpress

Automated MDS generation platform. With IMDS PLUS(or a2), utilizes BOM & received data to generate complex MDS structures.

• BOM-to-MDS: Insteant multi-tier tree structure generation.
• Omission Alerts: Supplier data gap analysis.
• Progress Tracking: Real-time project visualization dashboards.

SOC Analyst

Cloud-based substance assessment tool. Accelerates chemical data searching, querying, and restricted substance scanning.

• Instant Scan: Rapid substance and recyclability calculation.
• Custom Filters: Cross-referencing based on company-specific lists.
• Central DB: Material data consolidation for multiple plants.

GreenCheck

All-in-one product compliance platform. Manages product chemical compositions and automates global regulatory report creation.

• Global Coverage: REACH, RoHS, Prop65, PPWR, FMD and more
• BOM Scrubbing: Risks identication within BOM structures.
• Live Updates: Automatic database sync with the latest updates.
